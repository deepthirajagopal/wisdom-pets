Cards

Cards are used within this design system link the visitors to the projects. Cards include an image, keywords, project title, and summary of the content and most importantly link to the content.

Accessibility Considerations

To ensure accessibility, always include descriptive and meaningful alt text for card images and always style links as described in the typography section.

Usage

Use cards to showcase title and short description with images of projects to provide a good overview of work.
