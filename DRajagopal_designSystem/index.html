Deepthi Rajagopal Design System

This website presents the colours, typography, layout and major components of my portfolio website. I am a researcher who is organised, and creative. I like playful and simple design. This system incorporates the mentioned values in a subtle manner.

The system uses Bootstrap 5 framework, and is divided into three sections as below:

Foundations:

This section includes Typography, color, and logos.

Components:

This section includes Navigation, Buttons, and Cards.

Patterns:
