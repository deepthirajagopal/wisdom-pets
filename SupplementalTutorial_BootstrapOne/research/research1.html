Research: Medical Marijuana

Purpose of this study is to understand the user needs and design an app to support chronic pain patients with alternative medicine. To complete the study methods like literature review and interviews were used. 11 interviews were conducted with the users who have consumed marijuana in the past 3 months. Interviews also focused on understanding the reliable sources and their knowledge about Medical Cannabis. This study provides useful insights on the user needs that can be transformed into guidelines for designing a patient-centered app.
